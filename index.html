Restore Policy
  • Reverse mass immigration - Net-negative migration; undo the "Boriswave".
  • Deport all illegal migrants - Every person without permission removed (~2 million); mass deportations with hostile environment and 150,000–200,000 forced removals per year.
  • Abolish the asylum system - Illegal entry equals automatic rejection; no taxpayer-funded hotels.
  • Tent-based detention - Modular facilities on MoD/government land; mandatory detention pending deportation; no bail into the community.
  • Red List - Annual security-led review; stricter checks and limited visas for high-risk countries (e.g. Pakistan, India, Afghanistan, Eritrea, Sudan, Syria).
  • Deport foreign criminals - Automatic removal for any crime; rape-gang collaborators deported even through silence.
  • Deport on security grounds - Extremism and rejection of British values; Shamima's Law revokes dual-national extremists.
  • Fix the Afghan resettlement - Emergency arrivals relocated to safe third countries; Britain gave temporary refuge, not a permanent home.
  • Integration criteria - Must speak English, work, pay tax, and not claim benefits or live in social housing; failure means deportation.
  • Abolish ILR benefits - Time-limited, revocable residency conditional on contribution and good character.
  • Benefits for Brits only - Universal Credit, Child Benefit, and Housing Benefit reserved for British citizens.
  • Fairer spousal visas - Simplified fast-track for non-Red List countries; English fluency and self-sufficiency required.
  • Legal reforms - Leave the ECHR, repeal the Human Rights Act, pass a Great Clarification Act; harder path to citizenship.
